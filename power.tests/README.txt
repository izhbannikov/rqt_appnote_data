This folder contains results for power estimations of RQTests, QTests, SKAT and SKAT-O.

Folders:

- 'power.ld.cont' - contains scripts and results for continuous outcome in presence of 
linkage disequilibrium (LD).
- 'power.ld.dich' - contains scripts and results for dichotomous outcome in presence of 
linkage disequilibrium (LD).

Files:

- 'barplots_lasso.R', 'barplots_pca.R', 'barplots_pls.R', 'tables.R' - are scripts to 
make corresponding barplots.
